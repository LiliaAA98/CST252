A really short paragraph describing the task you picked from part 1

The task we decided to go with is a mirror simulator, where participants can place cubes on either side of a bar(mirror), and it reflects the cubes an equal distance on the other side.

Part one Answers

    What actions can I take?
  • Placing cubes
  • moving the mirror up and down
      • What do I need to remember or keep track of?
  • Keep track of the coordinates of the cube
  • Keep track of all the cubes placed
  • Keep track of the coordinates of the bar
      • What do I do over and over again?
  • Check for new cubes placed
  • Keep track of all the cubes placed
      • When do I need to make decisions?
  • Where on the y-axis the mirror is
  • Have to make the decision to subtract or add twice the distance between the cube and the mirror

    • Pseudocode Steps

  • Check position of mirror on the y-axis
  • Set cubes on screen, and check position of the cube that is placed
  • Keep track of the x-axis of the cube(it’s the same in the reflection)
  • Check distance between the cube and the mirror
  • If on top, subtract double the distance of the cube’s y-axis from the y-axis of the mirror. Add double if placed below
  • Add that value to the y-axis of the reflection (which should already have the x-axis from step 3)
